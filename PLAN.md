# Cooking Plan

We eat 5 meals a week:

Monday: 2 cup freezer
Tuesday: 2 cup freezer
Wednesday: 2 cup freezer
Thursday: 1 cup freezer
Saturday: 2 cup freezer

So how to prepare for this - given we cook once a week.

Since we're losing 4 2 cup meals and 1 1 cup meal a week

We run a cycle:

W1: 5 2 cup: 5 2 cup / 5 1 cup [Max 7]
W2: 5 2 cup: 6 2 cup / 4 1 cup [Max 8]
W3: 5 2 cup: 7 2 cup / 3 1 cup [Max 9]
W4: 5 2 cup: 8 2 cup / 2 1 cup [Max 10]
w5: 5 2 cup: 9 2 cup / 1 1 cup [Max 11]
w6: 5 1 cup: 5 2 cup / 5 1 cup 


So in total we need to have storage for 12 2 cup meals and 5 1 cup meals.

Most weeks we need to make 5 two cup meals or 5 1 cup meals.

Cycling meals:

Monday: Pasta + Pasta Sauce + Topping
Tuesday: Curry + Rice + Topping
Wednesday: Stir-fry + Noodles + Topping
Thursday: Dumplings + Veggies + Topping
Saturday: Meat and veggie mix + Bulgur/Couscous + Topping
